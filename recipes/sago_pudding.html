Sago Pudding

Description

AllRecipes recipe

Coconut tree sugar combined with coconut milk and poured over sago pearl pudding.

Ingredients

  • 10 cups of water
  • 7 ounces of pearl sago
  • 7 ounces of palm sugar
  • Half cup of water
  • 2 cups of canned coconut milk

Steps

  1. Boil 10 cups of water in a large pot. Slowly stir the sago pearls in to prevent clumping. Return to a boil then reduce the head to low. Cook until the sago turns translucent while occasionally stirring for 30 minutes. Pour the sago into jelly molds and refrigerate the molds overnight.
  2. In a small saucepan, stir the palm sugar and water. Bring to a boil until the sugar has dissolved. Strain through a fine sieve until sugar has dissolved.
  3. Unmold the refrigerated pudding. Serve with the coconut milk and palm sugar syrup separately for each consumer to pour over their pudding.
