Description

Kawaling Pinoy Recipe

Filipino-style beef steak dish usually served with rice. Thin sirloin slices braised in a mixture of citrus, soy sauce, onions, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds Beef sirloin
  • 2 lemons OR 4 calamansi
  • Quarter cup of soy sauce
  • 1 onion head
  • 1 garlic head
  • Quarter teaspoon of pepper
  • 3 tablespoons of canola oil
  • 1 cup of water

Steps

  1. Combine the beef, lemon juice, soy sauce, sliced onions, garlic, and pepper in a bowl. Massage the meat in the marinade and let marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Remove the meat, onions, and garlic then squeeze and save up the excess liquid.
  3. Heat oil in a pan at high heat. Add beef and cook for around 4 minutes per side until lightly browned. Save released juices.
  4. In the same pan, add onions and garlic. Stir regularly until softened, then return beef to pan.
  5. Add the marinade and meat juices back to the pan. Add water and build to a boil.
  6. Cover the pan, lower the heat, simmer for 40-50 minutes until meat is tender.
  7. Garnish the final dish with fresh onion rings.
